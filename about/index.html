About DogEmergency.org

DogEmergency.org is an educational resource that helps dog owners recognise emergency signs, reach a veterinarian or emergency animal hospital quickly, and understand the vet-guided recovery that follows. We focus on clear, calm, accurate triage guidance reviewed by our veterinary advisory board.

We are part of a network of pet-emergency resources operated under the CCH Foundation, a Hong Kong charity. Our guidance always puts veterinary care first.

Sources & standards

Emergency guidance follows AVMA, Merck Veterinary Manual, ASPCA Animal Poison Control, and small-animal emergency-medicine standards, reviewed by our veterinary advisory board.

DogEmergency.org is an educational resource and does not provide veterinary diagnosis or treatment. In an emergency, contact a veterinarian or emergency animal hospital immediately.
